How To Order

We bring shop to your finger tips

Step 1

Search the product that you want on search bar, or go through the list of categories & find the product you like.

Step 2

Click on the product that you want to buy.

Step 3

You have three options to order product By adding it to cart & buying it later: “Click to Add to Cart” By sending product query to us & we will respond very soon : “Click here to Order” By giving us just contact number & will will call you as soon as possible: ”Order by Phone”.

Step 3 Details

You can select multiple product & add it to cart & buy them all at once. When you click add to cart, to item then you are redirect to my cart page where all the product that you added to cart are listed you can check out from there by choosing payment method paypal, esewa or Global IME account or you can continune shopping by clicking on "continune shopping".
